Dr. Giuseppe Sutera obtained his Ph.D. in 2022 and conducts research in robotics in collaboration with the RoSys Group at the Department of Electrical, Electronic and Computer Engineering (DIEEI), University of Catania. His work focuses on autonomous multi-robot systems, including both terrestrial and aerial platforms, capable of navigating environments, executing pre-defined missions, and collaborating to achieve tasks beyond the capabilities of a single robot.
